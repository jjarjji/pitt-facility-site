About Pitt Facility Management LLC

Who We Are

Built on Experience. Driven by Results.

Pitt Facility Management LLC was founded with a clear mission: to provide Pittsburgh-area businesses with reliable, cost-effective facility management that goes beyond the call of duty. We are a full-service facility management company specializing in commercial and industrial properties.

Our team brings over 15 years of combined experience in electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and general maintenance trades. We are licensed in Pennsylvania, fully insured with $2M liability coverage, and EPA certified for HVAC work.

We serve office buildings, warehouses, manufacturing facilities, healthcare properties, retail spaces, and multi-family residential complexes across Allegheny County and the greater Pittsburgh metropolitan area.
